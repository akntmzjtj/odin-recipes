Banana Bread

Description

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Combine the dry ingredients in one bowl.
  2. Beat the butter and sugar in another bowl. Add the eggs and mashed bananas.
  3. Add the wet mixture to the dry mixture.
  4. Pour the batter into a loaf pan and bake in a preheated oven at 175Â°C

The banana bread should be cooked after an hour has passed. Let the bread sit in the pan for about 10 minutes before turning it out.
